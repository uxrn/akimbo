Akimbo Esports

We are an esports organization, club and media network based out of the United States.

Founded in August 2019, Akimbo started off as a simple passion project idea with a basic foundation for taking on the daunting world of professional esports.

Fast forward to today, we have competed against some of the top teams in a variety of different esports all across the world. We continue to push our limits and reach our milestone goals. Join us on our continued journey towards eternal esport greatness.
